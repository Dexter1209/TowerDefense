üéÆ Game Mechanics

  • Defenders can be placed on the grid to stop enemies from reaching the end of the path
  • Enemies spawn at regular intervals and move from right to left
  • Resources are earned by defeating enemies and can be used to purchase defenders
  • Each defender has unique abilities, attack ranges, and costs
  • Win by surviving long enough to reach the target score!
